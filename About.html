About us

Welcome to CTU-Buddy, your trusted companion in enhancing the learning experience at City Technical University (CTU). We're excited to introduce you to our platform and the passionate minds behind its creation.

Our Purpose:

At CTU-Buddy, our purpose is to revolutionize education by providing a comprehensive and interactive online platform that empowers students on their academic journey. We believe in making learning accessible, engaging, and collaborative, all while fostering a strong sense of community.

Our Vision:

Our vision is to redefine the way students learn and interact with educational resources. We strive to create a digital space where knowledge is shared, discussions flourish, and learning becomes an enriching experience for every CTU student.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly and innovative online ecosystem that simplifies academic life. We aim to enhance your learning through features like interactive timetables, collaborative discussions, resource sharing, and more. CTU-Buddy is designed to help you succeed academically while connecting you with a network of fellow learners.

Meet the Team:

Wilrich Van den Berg:

An avid learner and tech enthusiast, Wilrich brings a wealth of experience in web development and a deep understanding of user-centered design. He's driven by the belief that technology can revolutionize education and make it accessible to all.

Zandro Spinner:

With a passion for education and community building, Zandro has a background in educational psychology and instructional design. His goal is to create an environment where students can collaborate, share knowledge, and thrive academically.

Together, we embarked on the journey to create CTU-Buddy with the hope of making a positive impact on students' lives. We're committed to continuously improving and expanding the platform based on your feedback and needs.

Connect With Us:

We value your input and would love to hear from you. Whether you have questions, suggestions, or simply want to share your CTU-Buddy experience, feel free to reach out to us. You can connect with us via email: info@gmail.com, or through our contact form on the Contact Us page. Thank you for joining us on this exciting educational journey. Let's make learning at CTU an unforgettable experience together!

Sincerely,

Wilrich Van den Berg and Zandro Spinner

Co-Founders, CTU-Buddy
